Introducing Clara
Find the OFW messages that matter. Build the exhibit faster. Draft the abstract in minutes.
Clara helps family law attorneys and their teams turn long OFW message histories into court-ready work product. Start with the Abstract & Exhibits Builder. Tell Clara what issue you're looking for — missed exchanges, hostile communication, refusal to follow the parenting schedule, unpaid expenses, or another case pattern. Clara searches the client's OFW messages, pulls the relevant exchanges into an exhibit, and drafts a clear abstract for attorney review.

No more reading hundreds of messages line by line just to find the few that matter.
